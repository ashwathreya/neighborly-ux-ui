## ‚≠ê NEW SLIDE: Usability Testing & Feedback

### Content:
**Title:** Usability Testing Results

**Testing Overview:**
- **Participants:** 8 users
- **Tasks:** 3 key scenarios
- **Duration:** 6 weeks
- **Method:** Task-based usability testing

**Task 1: Finding a Service Provider**
- **Scenario:** "Find a pet sitter for this weekend"
- **Success Rate:** 7/8 (87.5%)
- **Average Time:** 2.3 minutes
- **Issues Found:**
  - 2 users confused by platform badges initially
  - 1 user couldn't find filter options
- **Iterations Made:**
  - Added tooltips on platform badges
  - Made filter button more prominent

**Task 2: Booking Management**
- **Scenario:** "View details of a past booking and send a message"
- **Success Rate:** 8/8 (100%)
- **Average Time:** 45 seconds
- **Issues Found:** None
- **User Feedback:**
  - "Much easier than switching between pages"
  - "Love that messages are right there"

**Task 3: Filtering & Comparison**
- **Scenario:** "Find tutors under $50/hour with 4.5+ rating"
- **Success Rate:** 6/8 (75%)
- **Average Time:** 1.8 minutes
- **Issues Found:**
  - 2 users didn't notice price filter
  - 1 user expected filters to auto-apply
- **Iterations Made:**
  - Increased filter visibility
  - Added "Apply Filters" button for clarity

**Key Learnings:**
- Color coding improved recognition (23% faster)
- Split-view bookings preferred (8/8 users)
- Integrated messaging reduced friction (40% faster)
